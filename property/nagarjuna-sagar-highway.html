About Property

This land parcel is located in Marriguda, on the Nagarjuna Sagar Highway, offering convenient connectivity while remaining set back from the main road. The land currently has an established Bathayi plantation, adding immediate agricultural value.

It is located approximately 20 km from the proposed Future City limits and around 3 km from the proposed Regional Ring Road (RRR) alignment. The location is well suited for long-term holding, combining agricultural usability with infrastructure-led growth potential.
